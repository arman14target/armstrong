Dallas-Fort Worth is one of America's largest gym markets — everything from Ronnie Coleman-era Metroflex to Uptown luxury towers. This guide ranks the **20 best gyms in Dallas** for bodybuilders, CrossFitters, and budget lifters across the Metroplex.

## How We Ranked Dallas Gyms

- **Drive time** — DFW sprawl makes location critical
- **AC and space** — Texas summers demand cold, spacious facilities
- **Equipment** — rack count, turf zones, cardio variety
- **Value** — Texas gyms love annual contracts; read terms
- **Culture** — bodybuilding heritage vs. boutique HIIT

> **Pricing note:** All costs below are **approximate estimates** for 2026 based on publicly listed rates, member reports, and typical promo pricing. Your actual price can differ by location, contract length, initiation fees, add-ons (parking, classes, annual fee), student/military discounts, and seasonal promotions. Always confirm current rates on the gym's website or in person before signing.
