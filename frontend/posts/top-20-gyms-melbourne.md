Melbourne's laneway culture extends to fitness — warehouse CrossFit boxes in Collingwood, premium towers on Collins Street, and suburban megaclubs in the outer ring. This guide ranks the **20 best gyms in Melbourne** for strength athletes, class lovers, and budget members.

## How We Ranked Melbourne Gyms

- **Tram and train access** — Melbourne's grid favours transit-friendly gyms
- **Equipment depth** — racks, platforms, specialty bars
- **Value** — weekly billing, no-lock-in options
- **Culture** — AFL city with strong CrossFit and powerlifting scenes
- **Weather** — indoor quality matters for wet winters

> **Pricing note:** All costs below are **approximate estimates** for 2026 based on publicly listed rates, member reports, and typical promo pricing. Your actual price can differ by location, contract length, initiation fees, add-ons (parking, classes, annual fee), student/military discounts, and seasonal promotions. Always confirm current rates on the gym's website or in person before signing.
