PureGym is the **largest gym operator in the United Kingdom** by club count — **500+ locations**, **24/7 access**, and **no-contract** memberships from roughly **£20/month** in the UK. It dominates Google searches for "cheap gym UK," "PureGym Ireland," and "no contract gym near me." This guide covers **PureGym pricing tiers**, **where clubs sit across England, Scotland, Wales, and Ireland**, and **what equipment** you get for the money.

## PureGym Membership Pricing (2026)

> **Pricing note:** All costs below are **approximate estimates** for 2026 based on publicly listed rates and typical promo pricing. Your actual price can differ by club tier (Off-Peak, Core, Plus, Extra), location (London premiums apply), and add-ons. Always confirm on [puregym.com](https://www.puregym.com) before joining.

| Tier | Est. Monthly Cost | Access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Off-Peak** | ~£20–£28/mo | Home club only, restricted hours (usually 10 AM–4 PM weekdays) |
| **Core / Standard** | ~£28–£40/mo | Home club, 24/7 |
| **Plus** | ~£35–£50/mo | Multiple clubs in your region |
| **Extra / Ultimate** | ~£45–£60/mo | Nationwide access + guest passes (tier names vary) |

### Common Add-Ons

- **Fixed-term discount** — 9- or 12-month commitment lowers monthly rate
- **Student discount** — ~10% off at participating clubs with valid ID
- **Joining fee** — often £0 during promos; otherwise ~£10–£20

PureGym's model is **pay monthly, cancel anytime** — a major draw versus legacy chains with 12-month lock-ins.

## Equipment at PureGym

## FAQ: PureGym

### How much is PureGym per month?

**Off-Peak ~£20–£28**, standard 24/7 **~£28–£40**, multi-club **~£35–£60** depending on region. Northern Ireland clubs often undercut mainland UK on promo pricing.

### Can I cancel PureGym anytime?

**Yes** — no-contract memberships cancel with one month's notice (typically). Fixed-term deals may have early exit fees.

### Does PureGym have free weights?

**Yes** — dumbbells, barbells, racks, and cables at all standard clubs.

### Is PureGym open 24 hours?

**Most UK and NI clubs are 24/7** for Core tier and above. Off-Peak memberships restrict entry hours.

### Is there PureGym in Dublin?

**Not yet in the Republic of Ireland** — PureGym's Irish clubs are in **Northern Ireland** (Belfast, Newry, Derry, and surrounds). Dublin lifters should compare [Dublin gym prices](/blog/top-20-gyms-dublin/) for local 24/7 options.

### PureGym vs The Gym Group — which is cheaper?

Often **The Gym Group** undercuts by ~£2–£5/month; PureGym wins on **club count, app, and Plus-tier refurbs** in major cities.
