PureGym is the **largest gym operator in the United Kingdom** by club count — **500+ locations**, **24/7 access**, and **no-contract** memberships from roughly **£20/month**. It dominates Google searches for "cheap gym UK" and "no contract gym near me." This guide covers **PureGym pricing tiers**, **where clubs sit across England, Scotland, and Wales**, and **what equipment** you get for the money.

## PureGym Membership Pricing (2026)

> **Pricing note:** All costs below are **approximate estimates** for 2026 based on publicly listed rates and typical promo pricing. Your actual price can differ by club tier (Off-Peak, Core, Plus, Extra), location (London premiums apply), and add-ons. Always confirm on [puregym.com](https://www.puregym.com) before joining.

| Tier | Est. Monthly Cost | Access |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Off-Peak** | ~£20–£28/mo | Home club only, restricted hours (usually 10 AM–4 PM weekdays) |
| **Core / Standard** | ~£28–£40/mo | Home club, 24/7 |
| **Plus** | ~£35–£50/mo | Multiple clubs in your region |
| **Extra / Ultimate** | ~£45–£60/mo | Nationwide access + guest passes (tier names vary) |

### Common Add-Ons

- **Fixed-term discount** — 9- or 12-month commitment lowers monthly rate
- **Student discount** — ~10% off at participating clubs with valid ID
- **Joining fee** — often £0 during promos; otherwise ~£10–£20

PureGym's model is **pay monthly, cancel anytime** — a major draw versus legacy chains with 12-month lock-ins.

## PureGym Locations Across the UK

PureGym targets **high-footfall urban and suburban sites** — retail parks, city centres, and transport hubs.

### Scotland

### Wales

Use the [PureGym club finder](https://www.puregym.com/gyms/) to filter by 24/7 status, women's-only zones (select clubs), and parking.

## Equipment at PureGym

PureGym floors are **compact but functional** — typically 8,000–15,000 sq ft in a retail unit. Equipment brand mix varies by build year.

### Free Weights & Racks

- Dumbbells — usually to **50 kg** (110 lbs) at larger clubs; smaller clubs may cap lower
- **2–4 squat racks or half racks** — peak-hour queues common in London
- Olympic barbells, bench stations (flat and incline)
- Smith machines at many locations

### Cardio & Machines

- Technogym, Life Fitness, or Matrix cardio lines
- Selectorized machines — full-body circuit
- Cable stations and functional rigs at newer **Plus-tier** refurbishments

### PureGym-Specific Features

| Feature | Notes |

- **Budget-conscious lifters** who want barbells without a contract trap

## FAQ: PureGym

### How much is PureGym per month?

**Off-Peak ~£20–£28**, standard 24/7 **~£28–£40**, multi-club **~£35–£60** depending on region.

### Can I cancel PureGym anytime?

**Yes** — no-contract memberships cancel with one month's notice (typically). Fixed-term deals may have early exit fees.

### Does PureGym have free weights?

**Yes** — dumbbells, barbells, racks, and cables at all standard clubs.

### Is PureGym open 24 hours?

**Most UK clubs are 24/7** for Core tier and above. Off-Peak memberships restrict entry hours.

### PureGym vs The Gym Group — which is cheaper?

Often **The Gym Group** undercuts by ~£2–£5/month; PureGym wins on **club count, app, and Plus-tier refurbs** in major cities.

PureGym owns the UK budget 24/7 segment. Pick **Off-Peak** only if your schedule fits; most lifters need **Core or Plus** for evening rack access. Scout your club at 6 PM before joining.
