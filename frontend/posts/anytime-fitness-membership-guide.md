Anytime Fitness built its brand on one promise: **train anytime**. With **5,000+ clubs** in 30+ countries and true **24/7 key-fob access**, it is one of the most-searched gym chains in English-speaking markets — the US, UK, Australia, New Zealand, and Canada. Clubs are smaller than LA Fitness but everywhere. Here is what membership costs, where to find locations, and what equipment fits inside.

## Anytime Fitness Membership Pricing (2026)

> **Pricing note:** All costs below are **approximate estimates** for 2026 based on publicly listed rates, member reports, and typical franchise pricing. Anytime Fitness is **franchise-owned** — prices vary significantly by owner and country. Confirm rates at your local club before signing.

| Cost Component | Est. Range (USD/GBP/AUD) |
| --- | --- |
| **Monthly dues** | ~$40–$60 / £35–£55 / A$50–A$70 |
| **Key fob / access fee** | ~$25–$50 one-time |
| **Enrollment** | ~$0–$100 (promo-dependent) |
| **Annual fee** | ~$35–$50 (some franchises) |

### What Membership Includes

- **24/7/365 access** via secure key fob (staffed hours vary)
- **Global reciprocity** — train at 3,000+ participating clubs worldwide with the same fob (Home club billing still applies)
- **Free fitness consultation** — initial session with a coach at many locations
- **Anytime Fitness App** — workout plans and class content

Personal training, tanning, and supplements are optional upsells — not included in base dues.

### Why Prices Vary So Much

Each club is independently owned. A suburban Minnesota Anytime may charge ~$35/month while a Manhattan or Sydney CBD location exceeds ~$70/month. Always get a written quote.

## Equipment at Anytime Fitness

Anytime clubs are typically **2,500–5,000 sq ft** — compact compared to LA Fitness or 24 Hour Fitness. Layout prioritizes essentials.

### Standard Equipment Line-Up

| Category | Typical Gear |
| --- | --- |
| **Cardio** | 8–15 treadmills, bikes, ellipticals, sometimes rower |
| **Free weights** | Dumbbells to ~50–100 lbs, adjustable benches |
| **Barbells** | 1–2 squat racks or half racks, Olympic bar, plates |
| **Machines** | Cable crossover, lat pulldown, leg press, selectorized circuit |
| **Functional** | TRX, kettlebells, medicine balls, turf strip (some clubs) |

### What to Expect (and Not Expect)

**Expect:** Enough to run a solid PPL or full-body program if you are not peak-hour dependent on the single squat rack.

**Do not expect:** Multiple power racks, posing room, pool, basketball court, or heavy dumbbells past 100 lbs at most locations.

Newer franchise builds often add a **small turf zone** for sled pushes and carries. Older clubs may feel tight during January rush.

## Who Anytime Fitness Is Best For

- **Shift workers** — nurses, warehouse, hospitality — who train at 3 AM
- **Suburban lifters** who want a key fob and no crowds at odd hours
- **Frequent travelers** who want reciprocal access across states or countries
- **Beginners** wanting a low-intimidation, small-floor environment

Less ideal for: powerlifters needing multiple racks, swimmers, or anyone who wants group class variety included free.

## FAQ: Anytime Fitness

### How much is Anytime Fitness per month?

**~$40–$60/month** in the US; **~£35–£55** in the UK; **~A$50–A$70** in Australia — plus possible key fob and annual fees.

### Is Anytime Fitness really 24/7?

**Yes** at most clubs for member key-fob entry. Staffed desk hours are shorter; security cameras monitor off-hours access.

### Can I use any Anytime Fitness with my membership?

**Global reciprocity** lets you visit thousands of partner clubs. Some owners restrict high-traffic tourist locations — ask your home club.

### Does Anytime Fitness have showers?

**Yes** — private shower stalls and lockers are standard in most builds.

### How do I cancel Anytime Fitness?

Cancellation policies are **franchise-specific**. Many require 30-day notice or buyout of remaining months. Read your contract and email your home club in writing.

## Bottom Line

Anytime Fitness trades floor space for **convenience and hours**. If your schedule does not fit 6 AM–10 PM commercial gyms, the key fob model wins. Visit at the hour you actually train, test rack availability, and log workouts in Armstrong — small clubs mean less redundancy when equipment is taken.
