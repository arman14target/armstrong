Brisbane's subtropical climate and river city layout create a unique gym market — outdoor bootcamps along the Brisbane River and air-conditioned megaclubs in the suburbs. This guide ranks the **20 best gyms in Brisbane** for strength athletes, HIIT lovers, and budget members.

## How We Ranked Brisbane Gyms

- **Train and bus access** — Brisbane's spread favours transit-connected gyms
- **Ventilation and AC** — humidity makes climate control essential
- **Equipment** — rack count and outdoor training options
- **Value** — weekly billing and no-lock-in options
- **Culture** — growing CrossFit scene and outdoor fitness culture

> **Pricing note:** All costs below are **approximate estimates** for 2026 based on publicly listed rates, member reports, and typical promo pricing. Your actual price can differ by location, contract length, initiation fees, add-ons (parking, classes, annual fee), student/military discounts, and seasonal promotions. Always confirm current rates on the gym's website or in person before signing.
