Vancouver combines ocean views, mountain backdrops, and some of Canada's highest fitness costs. From Yaletown towers to Burnaby warehouses, this guide ranks the **20 best gyms in Vancouver** for lifters, yogis, and budget members.

## How We Ranked Vancouver Gyms

- **SkyTrain access** — Vancouver's transit grid favours connected gyms
- **Equipment** — rack availability in premium vs. budget tiers
- **Value** — BC pricing runs higher than most Canadian cities
- **Rain-day readiness** — indoor quality matters 8 months a year
- **Culture** — yoga-forward city with growing strength scene

> **Pricing note:** All costs below are **approximate estimates** for 2026 based on publicly listed rates, member reports, and typical promo pricing. Your actual price can differ by location, contract length, initiation fees, add-ons (parking, classes, annual fee), student/military discounts, and seasonal promotions. Always confirm current rates on the gym's website or in person before signing.

## FAQ: Vancouver Gyms

### What is the best gym in Vancouver?

**Equinox** for luxury, **Fitness World** for chain convenience, and **Fittown Athletic Club** for hardcore training.

### How much does a gym membership cost in Vancouver?

Budget: **$20–$40/month**. Mid-tier: **$50–$90/month**. Premium: **$150–$300+/month**.

### Are there 24-hour gyms in Vancouver?

**Anytime Fitness**, **Snap Fitness**, and select **Fitness World** locations offer extended or 24/7 access.

### What is the cheapest gym in Vancouver?

**Club 16**, **Revo Fitness**, and **Jetts** consistently offer the lowest rates.
