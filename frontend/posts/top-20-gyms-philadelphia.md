## How We Ranked Philadelphia Gyms

- **SEPTA access** — subway and bus connectivity
- **Free-weight quality** — rack count and dumbbell range
- **Value** — Philly gyms often beat NYC pricing
- **Culture** — Rocky-era toughness meets modern boutiques
- **Hours** — early morning and late-night availability

> **Pricing note:** All costs below are **approximate estimates** for 2026 based on publicly listed rates, member reports, and typical promo pricing. Always confirm current rates on the gym's website or in person before signing.
